What is Dev{thon} 1.0

"Dev{thon} 1.0 " is a competition conducted by Leo Club of University of Moratuwa. It will be a dynamic web development competition for aspiring Sri Lanka developers to showcase their skills and creativity.

This competition aimed at promoting innovation and creativity in the field of full-stack web development and provides an opportunity for students to showcase their skills and knowledge in web development, and to learn from their peers in the process.

Register Now
Mission

Our Mission is to create an inclusive and collaborative environment where competitors from diverse backgrounds and varying levels of experience can come together to learn and grow. We aim to provide a platform for competitors to explore their creativity and apply their knowledge and skills to real-world challenges while fostering a sense of community and teamwork among participants.
